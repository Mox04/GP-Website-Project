Bangladesh

Summary of Major Social Program:
How it Works

The main objectives of the 7th five year plans fall under three categories: economy, poverty and labour. 6

Plans and goals of the 7th five year plan 6

GDP
- Averaging GDP growth of around 7.5% annually over the plan's 5 year period
Poverty
- Reduction in extreme poverty by about 4.0 percent
Labour
- Creating good jobs for the large pool of under-employed and new entrants to the labor force by increasing employment in the manufacturing sector from 15 percent to 20 percent

Impact

The percentage of people under the international $1.90 poverty line, poverty declined from 44.2 percent in 1991 to 13.8 percent in 2016/17. Net enrollment rate at the primary school level increased from 80 percent in 2000 to above 90 percent in 2015, and at the secondary school level increased from 45 percent in 2000 to around 62 percent in 2015. 7
