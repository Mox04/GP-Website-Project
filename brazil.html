Brazil

Summary of Major Social Program: Bolsa Familia
How it Works

Bolsa Família is a 'Conditional Cash Transfer Program' which provides financial aid to poor brazilian families. The conditional part of the program ensures that the children who the program aims to support, attend school and are vaccinated.6 48 million people are supported by the program.

Bolsa Família currently has two brackets:

  • $32 monthly - without a base payment7
  • $16 monthly - with a $16 base monthly payment7
The conditional payments are:
  • A monthly payment of $7.50 per vaccinated child who is attending school (below 16). Maximum of 5.7
  • $11 per youth (16/17 years old) attending school. Maximum of 2.7

Impact

In 2006 the Center for Political Studies of the Getulio Vargas Foundation has published a study showing the reduction of poverty from “28.2% in 2003 to 22.7% last year, a reduction of 19.18%”8
