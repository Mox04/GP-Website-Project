Poland

Summary of Major Social Program: Family 500+
How it Works

The program is a monthly, tax-free payment a family receives for every child, regardless of income, employment status and other sources of child benefit, below the age of 18 (Before August 2019, every child starting from the second). The payment is 500 PLN ($128.10) per child and is received by bank transfer.6

Impact

Although not a program directed for helping people in poverty, the Polish Family 500+ program has had the greatest impact on the Polish underage citizens living in poverty. In December of 2018 (before the expansion of the program) the number of payments sent was 3,585,800.6 Evidence connecting the decrease of children in poverty with the start of the 500+ programs shows that the program has had a powerful impact on the number. By one third to be exact.6
