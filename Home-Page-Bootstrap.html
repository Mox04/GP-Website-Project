Social programs supporting people in poverty

Research Question

How do different cultures support people in poverty
To make researching easier, only the main, governmentally run, social program supporting people in poverty was used for each country.

One country from each continents was researched:

  • N. America - Canada
  • S. America - Brazil
  • Europe - Poland
  • Africa - Botswana
  • Asia - Bangladesh
The Oceania continent was omited from research.
