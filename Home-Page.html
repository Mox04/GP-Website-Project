GP Project - Social Programs supporting People in Poverty

Comparison

Brazil Canada Poland Botswana Bangladesh
Research Question
How do different cultures support people in poverty

To make researching easier, only the main, govermentaly run, social program supporting people in poverty was used for each country.

One country from each continents was researched:
  • N. America - Canada
  • S. America - Brazil
  • Europe - Poland
  • Africa - Botswana
  • Asia - Bangladesh
The Oceania continent was omited from research.
